Rules

  • Player 1 always stats the game first
  • Hit "ROLL DICE" to roll the dice
  • Score from the rolled dice will appear to your current score
  • You can choose to roll as many times as they want, as long as its your trun
  • You can loose your turn by rolling "1" on one or both of your dice
  • If "1" is rolled on one of your dice, you loose your current score
  • If "1" is rolled on both of the dice, you loose your total score.
  • If both dices are the same other then "1" and "1", the score rolled is multipled by 2.
  • You can choose to hold your curent score at any time during your turn
  • Hitting hold will save your curent score to your total score, but you will loose your turn
  • First player that will reach the set goal with their total points will win the game
